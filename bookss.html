My Favorite Book: It Starts with Us

About the Book

"It Starts with Us" is a contemporary romance novel by Colleen Hoover. This sequel to "It Ends with Us" continues Lily Bloom's journey as she faces new beginnings, explores healing, and navigates complex relationships.

Why I Love It

I love this book for its heartfelt storytelling and the way it provides closure and hope for the characters. The narrative is engaging and offers a powerful message about growth and second chances.
